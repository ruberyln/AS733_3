Guidelines for interaction

Perspective showing the inside of a cube with various colors.

To move the object farther and view shapes click "Farther button"

To move the object close and view shapes click "Nearer button"

To view a smaller version of object click "Smaller"

To view a bigger version of object click "Bigger"

To turn object in a anti-clockwwise manner "Anti-ClockWise Rotation"

To turn object in a clockwwise manner "ClockWise Rotation"

To rotate the object around towards you click "Rotate Forward button"

To rotate the object in an opposite direction click " Rotate Back button"
